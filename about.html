Meet The Women Behind the Most Creative Event

The PARTY Project is a company made up of creative women, aiming to make everyone celebrate their day to its fullest. We create events with impeccable detail and care to our clients' priorities. From Full Service Event Planning to In-house Preparations, we treat life as a PARTY.

STELLA

PARTY BOSS, SUPERMOM, CAT LOVER

I grew up in Bulacan, but my very broad childhood imagination kept me dreaming of a life in Makati. In 2011, I made that dream a reality by landing a job in IT at SGS. Shortly after I moved to Makati, I met my event partner. That’s when I made the decision to start a business revolving around my dream of partying for a living. So The PARTY Project was born - a unique event planning and styling company that focuses on the special details tailored to each of our clients. In order to make Colors and Details successful it was imperative to build a team who could join the party with enthusiasm, dynamic personalities and creativity. Here we are today, a group of incredible women who live and love to party.

COFOUNDER, ULTIMATE FOODIE, FLORAL GURU

Ingrid was born and raised in Manila. Her love for parties began at a young age while working at a family owned flower shop where she designed floral arrangements for a variety of events. She later attended De La Salle University where she majored in Art and minored in Entrepreneurship. In her final year of school, she met Stella and couldn’t have been happier. Outside of The PARTY Project, Natalie is a huge foodie, always finding new places to explore her tastes. She enjoys indulging in a good Netflix show and spending time with loved ones.
